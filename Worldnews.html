Top 20 Islamic Scholars in History

There are very few religions in the world that were spread through the work of worthy scholars. Here is the list of Islamic Scholars in History who created a long lasting impact.

1. Ibn Sina (Avicenna) (980–1037)

2. Al-Farabi (872–950)

3. Ibn Rushd (Averroes) (1126–1198)

4. Ibn Khaldun (1332–1406)

5. Al-Ghazali (1058–1111)

6. Ibn Taymiyyah (1263–1328)

7. Ibn Kathir (1301–1373)

8. Al-Tabari (839–923)

9. Ibn Qayyim al-Jawziyya (1292–1350)

10. Al-Razi (865–925)

11. Al-Biruni (973–1048)

12. Ibn al-Haytham (965–1040)

13. Al-Fakhr al-Razi (1149–1209)

14. Ibn Battuta (1304–1368)

15. Al-Mawardi (972–1058)

16. Al-Firdausi (935–1020)

17. Ibn Hazm (994–1064)

18. Al-Jahiz (776–868)

19. Ibn al-Nafis (1213–1288)

20. Ibn al-Jazari (1136–1206)
